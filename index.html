How It Works

From unstructured document to structured, actionable data in four steps.

1

Ingest

Documents arrive via upload, API, or cloud storage and enter the processing queue.

2

Classify

AI identifies the document type and routes it to the appropriate extraction pipeline.

3

Extract

OCR and LLMs pull structured fields from the document. Operators review and correct in a purpose-built interface.

4

Deliver

Validated data is output as EDI 835, HL7, JSON, or pushed directly into downstream systems via API.

Solutions

Purpose-built for the workflows that matter most in healthcare.

Revenue Cycle

Extract claims, adjustments, and payment data from insurance remittance documents. Generate EDI 835 files and transmit electronically — replacing manual data entry from paper EOBs.

Imaging & Clinical Orders

Capture patient demographics, ICD-10 codes, and ordering details from imaging order forms across EHR systems like Epic, Athena, and eClinicalWorks. Analyze radiology reports to surface follow-up recommendations and incidental findings.

Knowledge Extraction
